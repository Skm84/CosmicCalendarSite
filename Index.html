Problem and Solution

As we move into an age where the logical next step in space exploration is not only putting probes on further planets but also putting people there—especially with a manned mission to Mars sooner rather than later—the question of “how do we schedule data transfer or meetings?” arises. Our solution is a system that takes a date and a time from any one planet and adjusts it for the timezone, returning a new date and time on the other planet. This would allow us to standardize timing among planets and reduce friction in scheduling and data transfer.
